Our Story

A Legacy Woven
Into Every Thread

Founded by Michelle George, The Tailor's Daughter is a tribute to her father Foster George — a master tailor whose 50-year career spanned two continents and touched countless lives.

Growing up in her father's factories, Michelle developed a discerning eye for quality and an appreciation for understated elegance. Every item in our collection has been personally curated with both Caribbean residents and our beloved visitors in mind.

From the frugal traveler to the worldly jet setter, our hand-selected pieces from sea island cotton weavers, salt miners, and pottery makers represent the finest the Caribbean has to offer.

Michelle George Founder & CEO
